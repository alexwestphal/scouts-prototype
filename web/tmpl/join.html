For more than 100 years, Scouts New Zealand has brought a world of adventure, outdoor experience and friendship to countless New Zealand youth.

Scouting makes a difference. Providing opportunities for youth to grow, experience new "firsts" and develop into citizens of the world.

Scouts demonstrate self-confidence and optimism. They express a strong sense of civic responsibility and have a desire to give back to the community. They become more physically fit, energetic, outgoing, conscientious and compassionate. Plus they have a lot of fun!

But why let kids have all the fun? Scouts New Zealand relies on its network of more than 3,000 caring and dedicated volunteers to deliver its programming to youth. And there is always room for more.

So whether you are looking to register your son or daughter in one of Scouts New Zealand’s five fun and challenging programs, looking to find a rewarding volunteering experience or both, you have found the right place.
