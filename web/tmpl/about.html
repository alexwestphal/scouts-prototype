About Scouts New Zealand

With more than 15,000 members, Scouts New Zealand is the nation’s premier youth-serving organization.

With every new member, with every camp and every community service project, Scouts New Zealand makes one simple promise to New Zealand youth, parents and society:

Scouts have fun adventures
discovering new things and experiences
they wouldn’t discover elsewhere.
Along the way they develop into capable,
confident and well-rounded individuals,
better prepared for success in the world.
Scouts is the start of something great.
It starts with Scouts.

Scouts New Zealand’s national office is located in Wellington. Scouts New Zealand is a not-for-profit organization and a member of the World Organization of the Scout Movement.
