Ways To Give

There are three easy ways to make an individual donation to Scouts New Zealand

Donate Online:

Save a stamp! Donate through our secure online donation form here.

Donate by Phone:

Give us a ring! Donate by phone. Call us at 0800-SCOUTS (0800-726887) Monday to Friday, 8:00 am to 4:00 pm.

Donate by Mail or Fax:

Go traditional! Download and print our Donation Form and mail or fax it to:

Scouts New Zealand
1 Kaiwharawhara Rd
Kaiwharawhara, Wellington 6035

There are many other ways you can contribute to Scouts New Zealand and benefit children and youth around the country.

Become a Monthly Donor

A monthly donation is one of the best ways to support Scouts New Zealand and our mission. Monthly donations help us to plan funding efficiently and is very cost effective. For as little as 1$ a day you can bring the joys of Scouting to children and youth throughout New Zealand. Your donation will be processed on the 15th of every month and you will receive a consolidated tax receipt for your combined annual gifts each January.

Recognize a Leader and Local Hero

Has a leader made a difference in the life of your child? What a great way to say thank you to his or her leader by making a special gift in recognition of their hard work. We will send a card to the leader being recognized to acknowledge their dedication and hard work. Click here to nominate a Local Hero today.

Continue your Legacy

Your legacy can help to provide a place where kids can be themselves, can learn new skills, and grow into healthy functional members of society. Leaving a charitable gift in your Will is one way you can ensure that the principles that matter most to you are upheld after you are gone.

There are many ways to give a planned gift, through your Will and bequests, securities, trusts, bonds and annuities. You can even leave personal property or real estate. Please let us know if you name Scouts New Zealand in your estate plans. By letting us know ahead of time, we can make sure your gift is used in the way you wish it to be used, including the possibility of establishing an endowment fund in your name. Contact us for more information here.
